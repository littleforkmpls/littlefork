About Me

Hi I'm Tony. I'm a passionate & professional software engineer with 20+ years of experience planning, designing, & building websites. I choose technology on the basis of what’s right for my clients, not what’s trendy for developers. I hold pragmatism in the highest regard and detest over-engineering. And most of all, I value being a good citizen of the web by making it work for people regardless of who they are, where they are, or whatever device they have in front of them.
